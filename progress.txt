## Codebase Patterns
- Use `@/` alias for imports (configured in vite.config.ts and tsconfig.json)
- All scenes extend Phaser.Scene and use `{ key: 'SceneName' }` in constructor
- Scene keys follow pattern: 'BootScene', 'MainMenuScene', 'WorldScene', etc.
- Phaser game config: 1920x1080 resolution, FIT scaling, CENTER_BOTH
- Color scheme: Gold primary (#D4A84B), dark background (#1a1a2e)
