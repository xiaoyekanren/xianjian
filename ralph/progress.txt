## Codebase Patterns
- Map locations have: id, name, displayName, maps[], shops[], inns[], chapter?, description?
- Maps have: id, name, displayName, description, locationId, chapter, width, height, tileWidth, tileHeight, colorTheme, environment, layers[], npcs[], transitions[], events[], collisions[]
- Map layer types: ground, building, overlay, event
- TileType values: 0=GROUND, 1=WALL, 2=WATER, 3=DOOR, 4=PATH, 5=TRANSITION, 6=NPC_SPAWN, 7=TREE, 8=BUILDING, 9=FENCE, 10=CHEST, 11=EVENT
- Transitions have: id, targetMapId, targetX, targetY, sourceX, sourceY, direction
- Events have: id, type, x, y, triggerType, triggerOnEnter/triggerOnInteract, dialogId, requireFlag, setFlag, oneTime, priority
