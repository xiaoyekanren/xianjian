## Codebase Patterns
- Use `loadSkillsFromJson()` to parse skills.json data
- Cast JSON imports through `unknown` for type safety with nullable fields
- Story battles use `canFlee: false` to prevent escape from story encounters
- Boss enemy IDs follow pattern: `[character]_boss` (e.g., `lin_yueru_boss`)
- Enemy drops reference item IDs that must exist in items.json
